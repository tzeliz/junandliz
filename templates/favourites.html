FAVOURITES

As many of our friends and family are visiting from out of town, we’ve put together a map of Jun’s personal favourite spots around Penang. Some pins include personal notes and helpful tips, so be sure to check the descriptions.
A few things to keep in mind: most hawker centres are cash-only, the weather will be hot and humid (with a chance of rain), and Grab is usually the most convenient way to get around unless you have your own car. Enjoy exploring!
